About Company

  • MARS Asset Reconstruction Services., is a professional and reliable asset reconstruction company that provides effective solutions to businesses and financial institutions facing non-performing assets. The company has a team of experienced professionals who are committed to delivering high-quality services and helping clients achieve their financial goals. MARS Asset Reconstruction Services is a trusted partner for companies looking to resolve their asset-related challenges.

  • The Company with its appropriate credentials offers a wide array of services to help Banks and Financial Institutions in resource mobilization and to realize maximum value out of their Non Performing Assets (NPAs). It aims to become the most influential advisor in this business segment backed with right information, knowledge and their application.

  • The company is working with a strong Team of professionals from diverse background such as Administrators, Bankers, Chartered Accountants, Finance Professionals, Lawyers, Valuers, etc., each bringing their rich and valuable experience to the table. Growing opportunities in this business segment is expected to keep the team always challenged, owing to the uniqueness of each case which when resolved translate into the finest results.

  • Based on decades of experience, The Company is equipped with professionally experienced team, with rich collective experience in the areas of NPA management. These qualified debt recovery managers bring all the desired results through effective analysis of each factor that stall the recovery process of distressed receivables and finding resolutions for the same.
